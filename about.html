About Us

Gentlemen Pieces is Toronto’s go-to source for customized and modified timepieces. Built with passion and precision, our watches are reimagined Seiko builds that blend vintage aesthetics with modern craftsmanship.

Each build is hand-assembled, individually tuned, and customized to fit your unique style. We aren't affiliated with Seiko — we just love building off great foundations and making them our own.
